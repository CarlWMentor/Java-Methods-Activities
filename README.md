# Java-Methods-Activities
5 Activities: 
1. **Daily Water Intake Tracker** - A simple health program that tracks how many glasses of water a person drinks during the day.
2. **Restaurant Bill Calculator** - A simple program to calculate the total cost of a customer's meal.
3. **Fuel Cost Estimator** - Estimates the fuel cost of a trip.
4. **Monthly Budget Checker** - Calculates the total monthly expenses and determines whether you're spending within budget.
5. **Shop Change Calculator** - Calculates change after customer pays for an item.
6. **Shop Change Calculator With Added VAT15%**
